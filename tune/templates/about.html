About

In July 2023, Tune Collector was brought to life by Chris Grubb and Bennett Carpenter.

Chris, a visionary in user experience, crafted the intricate user models responsible for seamless account registration and login. Additionally, he developed user-only access to our extensive databases, opening a world of musical exploration for our users whilst protecting their personal information.

Complementing Chris's technical expertise, Bennett Carpenter infused Tune Collector with captivating design elements. With meticulous attention to detail, Bennett created an aesthetically stunning environment while ensuring the site's responsiveness across various screens.

Join us on Tune Collector, where you can track your old time tunes easier.
